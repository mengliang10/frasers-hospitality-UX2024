About London

London is one of the world's great cities — a vibrant, ever-evolving metropolis that weaves centuries of history through a dynamic modern landscape. From the majestic sweep of the Thames to the storied lanes of the East End, London rewards the curious and the discerning in equal measure.

Whether you're in the capital for business or pleasure, the city's world-class museums, iconic landmarks, acclaimed restaurants and extraordinary cultural scene make every day an adventure. Our London properties put you right at the heart of it all.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know

What's the best area to stay in London?
−
It depends on your priorities. Canary Wharf is ideal for business travellers — well-connected, modern and efficient. Kensington offers a calmer, more residential feel with proximity to museums and parks. Clerkenwell is perfect for those who want to be close to the creative heart of the city.
How do I get from Heathrow Airport to my property?
+
The fastest option from Heathrow is the Elizabeth Line (30–40 minutes to central London). Taxis take approximately 45–60 minutes depending on traffic. Our concierge team can arrange a private transfer for you in advance.
Can I park at your London properties?
+
Parking availability varies by property. Fraser Place Canary Wharf has limited on-site parking available at an additional charge. We recommend contacting the property directly or using one of the nearby public car parks.
Is public transport easy to use in London?
+
London has one of the world's best public transport networks. The Underground (the Tube), Overground, Elizabeth line and extensive bus network make it easy to reach virtually any part of the city. We recommend getting an Oyster card or simply using a contactless card for all journeys.
What is the cancellation policy?
+
Our flexible rate bookings can typically be cancelled up to 24–48 hours before arrival without charge. Advance purchase rates are non-refundable. Exact policies vary by property and rate type — please review the booking conditions when reserving.
Are pets allowed at your London properties?
+
Pet policies vary by property. Some of our London residences do welcome well-behaved dogs with advance notice and a small additional charge. Please contact the property directly to confirm pet-friendly availability before booking.
