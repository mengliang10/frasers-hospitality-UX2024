About this property

A refined residence in the heart of Glasgow

Fraser Suites Glasgow occupies a stunning conversion of a Grade A listed Victorian warehouse in the historic Merchant City district. Beautifully blending original architectural features with contemporary interiors, the property offers 99 studio and one-bedroom apartments perfectly suited to both short breaks and extended stays.

Each apartment features a fully equipped kitchen, separate living area, high-speed Wi-Fi and satellite television. Guests enjoy access to the state-of-the-art fitness centre and complimentary 24-hour concierge service.

Getting here

Perfectly placed in Merchant City

Fraser Suites Glasgow sits in the vibrant Merchant City district, just moments from the best restaurants, bars and cultural attractions Glasgow has to offer. Glasgow Central Station is a 10-minute walk away.

  • Glasgow Central Station 0.7 km • 10 min walk
  • Glasgow City Centre Shopping 0.5 km • 7 min walk
