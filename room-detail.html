About this room

The Studio Deluxe at Fraser Suites Glasgow is a beautifully designed studio apartment set within a Grade A listed Victorian warehouse in Glasgow's vibrant Merchant City district. Exposing the building's original architectural details — from stripped sandstone walls to the high timber-beamed ceilings — the Studio Deluxe seamlessly blends heritage character with contemporary luxury.

At 36 square metres, this is our most popular studio option, offering more space than the Classic, along with a fully equipped kitchen rather than a kitchenette. Whether you're preparing a home-cooked meal or simply making a morning coffee, the sleek kitchen provides everything you need.

The king-size bed features premium mattress and linen for the finest night's sleep, while the marble bathroom with rain shower completes the picture of understated luxury.
